How to increase my score?

You may visit the store and purchase upgrades or recruit more developers to increase the rate at which you gain points.

Additionally, you may click the computer to collect points.

How to save the game?

The game will automatically save your progress; however, if you would like to ensure that it is up to date before leaving, you may press the save button.

Conversely, if you want to begin a new game, press the "New" button and accept the prompt.
