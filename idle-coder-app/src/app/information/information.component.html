Development Team

Idle Coder was created by North Dakota State University students Victoria Neustel, Landon Raph, Steven Posterick, and Trenton Sanford for their CSCI 313 final project.

Development Process

Idle Coder was based off existing idle games like Cookie Clicker by Julien Thiennot. In general, idle games revolve around user input adding to the score.

We chose this idea based on each of our interests in video games and decided to make our final project based around the topic instead of choosing a topic we weren't as interested in.
